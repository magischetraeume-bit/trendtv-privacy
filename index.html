TrendTV – Privacy Policy Last updated: December 2025 Thank you for using TrendTV. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how your data is protected. 1. No Personal Data Collected TrendTV does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. The app does not request or process: name email address phone number exact location (GPS) photos or media contacts account information device ID TrendTV can be fully used without registration and without creating any user profile. 2. Advertising (Google AdMob) TrendTV displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect anonymized technical data, such as: approximate location (region or country) device type, model, OS version, language usage statistics needed for ad delivery Google Advertising ID These data are handled exclusively by Google, according to their privacy policies. Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy You can reset or disable your device’s advertising ID at any time in the Android settings. 3. Children’s Privacy The app includes an optional age selection to adjust ad categories (kids, teens, adults), but no personal data is collected, even from users under 18. TrendTV follows all Google Play rules for: child-safe apps restricted advertising categories 4. No Data Storage or External Servers TrendTV does not operate a cloud database or backend server. The app does not upload or store any user information externally. All settings and preferences stay only on the device. 5. Security Since TrendTV does not handle personal data, the privacy risk for users is minimal. For advertising components, Google AdMob uses industry-standard security, encryption, and fraud protection. 6.
